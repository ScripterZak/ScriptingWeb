About

I build gameplay-first Roblox systems with a strong focus on responsiveness, readability, and practical structure. I like shipping fast, but I also care about organization and long-term maintainability.

What I focus on

Combat, round systems, roleplay interactions, simulators, tycoons, UI systems, and prototype-to-portfolio builds. I aim for systems that are playable, recordable, and easy to expand later.

How I work

I prefer building full working systems instead of tiny disconnected snippets. That means clear structure, practical server-client separation, and features that can actually be shown in a real Studio recording.
